SWordMap: Learning Semantically Related Words in Software through Word Embedding

Introduction

We publish our mined semantically related words for any scientific work of other people.

Mined Semantically Related Words

  • Sample Results

    We randomly sample 100 words from the results trained from 343MB StackOverflow questions tagged with "java", manually check the correctness of the top 10 close words, and categorize the correct results into 6 groups. We also check how many of them cannot be found in WordNet or Merriam-webster english dictionary. Two authors verify the results in order to reduce subjectivity.
